What do I need to do?

First of all, you're going to need your survey topic and general questions/answers related to it decided. What is special about being a simple survey is that each survey will be limited to a maximum of 10 questions, and each question will have anything from 2 to 5 multiple choice answers. No typing or written answers allowed!

Why not?

The main reason of having these special rules for simple surveys is to let the user who is completing the survey for you know that it will be a very fast survey. No "it will take only 15 minutes of your time" stuff here! Each simple survey should be able to be completed by a maximum of 5 minutes, for those who really want to read and answer each question properly. Other's will be easily finished in a matter of seconds. What we want to achieve with our simple surveys is the guarantee that the survey will be a fast one!
